2048

Join the numbers & shapes to get to 2048 tile.

HOW TO PLAY: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number and at least one matching symbol touch, they merge into one.

Created by Veera Sundar. Inspired from 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
